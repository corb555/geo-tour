Flood Basalt Province

Long before the modern mountains rose, the earth here tore open. Massive fissures in the crust unleashed a Flood Basalt event.

Unlike a standard volcano, this was a deluge of liquid rock. Flow after flow piled up, layer upon layer, until the ancient landscape was buried under a flat, frozen ocean of black basalt over a mile thick.
