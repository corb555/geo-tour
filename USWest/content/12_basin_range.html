Horst and Graben Topography

The Basin and Range Province is the result of Crustal Extension. Tensional stress is pulling the crust apart, causing it to thin and fracture.

This occurs along Normal Faults, where blocks of crust slide downward relative to each other. The uplifted blocks form the ranges (Horsts), while the down-dropped blocks form the sediment-filled basins (Grabens).

The total extension here has stretched the continent by nearly 100% of its original width, bringing the hot mantle closer to the surface.
