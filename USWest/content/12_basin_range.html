Crustal Extension

In Nevada, the crust is being pulled apart. This is known as Crustal Extension.

As the crust stretches, it cracks into massive blocks. Some blocks tilt up to form mountain Ranges , while others drop down to form Basins.

The flat valleys you see today are deceptive; they're actually deep tectonic chasms filled with sediment. If you removed the sediment, the mountains would rise almost 10,000 feet straight up from the basin floor.
