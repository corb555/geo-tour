Crustal Extension

In Nevada, the Earth is being pulled apart. This is known as Crustal Extension.

As the crust stretches, it cracks into massive blocks. Some blocks tilt up to form mountain Ranges , while others drop down to form Basins.

However, the flat valleys you see today are deceptive; they have been filled with sediment eroded from the peaks. If you removed that fill, you would see deep tectonic chasms. The mountains wouldn't just be 4,000 feet tall; they would be raw cliffs rising 8,000 to 10,000 feet straight up from the basement floor.
